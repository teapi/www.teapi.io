About

Teapi exposes your data as a modern and powerful API, enabling you to build mobile applications and websites, connect with users and integrate with systems. Getting started with Teapi is easy. First you define your data model - similar to creating a database schema - next you upload your data, and then you query the API - sorting and filtering however you want.

Features

Use Your Existing System

Treat Teapi as an extension of your system, integrating it with little or no change to existing code.

Fast and Reliable

With Teapi, your data is replicated to multiple continents, ensuring low latency and high availability.

Security

Restrict access to your data or make it open to the public; the choice is yours.
